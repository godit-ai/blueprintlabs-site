# PMI Executive Summary

## 🎯 The Innovation

**Personal Model Identity (PMI)** replaces static AI context with learned model weights that encode personal patterns as dense mathematical vectors. This breakthrough achieves **98% token compression** while maintaining full personalization quality.

## 💡 The Problem

Current AI systems send thousands of tokens of repeated context with every interaction:

```
Traditional: "Clayton Jeanette, 32, systems engineer with MBA from Denver, 
founder of Blueprint Labs working on Gift Genie, Legacy Energy..." [7,000 tokens]

PMI: [0.72, -0.31, 0.85, ...] [50 tokens equivalent]
```
